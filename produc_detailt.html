BALL PEN 1423

The Ball Pen 1423 is a classical ball pen with a ventilated security cap. Of course it provides smooth writing properties and a high colour intensity ink for perfect writing results. Available in tinted colour or transparent barrels and with 3 different ink colours (red, blue and black). Point sizes: 0.5, 0.7 and 1.0 mm.
